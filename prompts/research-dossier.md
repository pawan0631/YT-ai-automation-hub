# Research Dossier Prompt

Use this prompt after collecting sources.

```text
You are a documentary research producer. Build a source-backed research dossier for a 15-18 minute YouTube documentary.

Topic: {{TOPIC}}
Channel niche: {{CYBER_OR_BUSINESS}}
Target audience: English-speaking viewers interested in high-stakes documentary storytelling.

Use only the provided sources. If a claim is uncertain, mark it as unverified.

Output:
1. One-paragraph story premise.
2. Why viewers should care.
3. Timeline of key events.
4. Main people/organizations.
5. Highest-stakes facts with source references.
6. Contradictions or weak evidence.
7. Visual/b-roll suggestions.
8. Potential title angles.
9. Compliance risks.
10. Open questions for human review.
```

# Content Workflow

## Weekly Cadence

For two channels, target one long documentary and two supporting shorts per channel per week only after the format is stable.

## Episode Pipeline

1. Topic scoring: curiosity, stakes, authority, visual potential, freshness, compliance risk.
2. Source capture: save reports, articles, papers, filings, and videos in Zotero/Paperless.
3. Research dossier: timeline, characters, claims, evidence, contradictions, visuals.
4. Script: hook, setup, escalation, turning point, consequence, lesson.
5. Fact-check: verify dates, names, claims, numbers, and attributions.
6. Production: voiceover, b-roll, graphics, thumbnail, shorts.
7. Compliance: copyright, synthetic disclosure, cyber safety, defamation risk.
8. Upload: title, description, chapters, tags, pinned comment, end screen.
9. Analytics: CTR, first 30 seconds retention, average view duration, comments, subs gained.

## Human Approval Points

Do not fully automate topic choice, final script approval, fact-checking, title/thumbnail choice, or final upload decision.

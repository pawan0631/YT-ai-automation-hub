# YouTube Compliance Notes

## Cybersecurity Content

Allowed direction: education, defense, history, risk analysis, incident response, policy, geopolitics, and business impact.

Avoid:

- Actionable exploit walkthroughs.
- Credential theft, phishing, evasion, malware deployment, persistence, or bypass instructions.
- Sharing live targets, stolen data, or abuse-ready commands.
- Encouraging cyber harm.

## Business and Personality Content

- Attribute claims to credible sources.
- Separate facts from analysis.
- Avoid unsupported allegations.
- Use careful wording for lawsuits, fraud, sanctions, bankruptcies, and personal conduct.

## AI/Synthetic Media

- Track which assets are AI-generated.
- Disclose realistic synthetic scenes where platform rules require it.
- Avoid making generated scenes look like real documentary evidence unless clearly labeled.

## Copyright

- Use licensed, public domain, owned, or fair-use-transformative material with judgment.
- Keep source/license notes in the episode brief.
